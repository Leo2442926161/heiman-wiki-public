In 2022, Heiman Technology was officially recognized as a National Specialized and New "Little Giant" Enterprise for its deep technical accumulation and innovation in the smart sensor field.

{/* truncate */}

This national-level recognition affirms Heiman's excellence in specialization, refinement, differentiation, and innovation. Heiman will continue to deepen its efforts in Smart Fire Safety, Smart Gas Safety, Smart Elderly Care, and Smart Sensors, consistently delivering high-quality products and services to customers.

Heiman had previously received recognition as a National High-Tech Enterprise and a Guangdong Province Specialized and New SME.
