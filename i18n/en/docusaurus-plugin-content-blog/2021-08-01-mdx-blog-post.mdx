Heiman Technology's new generation Matter protocol smart smoke alarm, the HS1SA-M, has been officially launched — one of the industry's first smoke alarms to receive Matter 1.2 certification.

{/* truncate */}

:::tip

The HS1SA-M supports Apple HomeKit, Amazon Alexa, Google Home, and Samsung SmartThings — the four major smart home ecosystems.

:::

## Key Features

- **Matter 1.2 Certified** — Cross-brand, cross-ecosystem interoperability
- **Photoelectric Smoke Sensing** — Rapid response to various fire smoke types
- **Voice Alarm** — Multiple language options, clear hazard alerts
- **Self-Test Function** — Regular self-checks to ensure normal operation
- **Remote Notification** — Real-time push alerts to your phone APP

## Compatibility

The HS1SA-M seamlessly integrates with major smart home platforms without requiring an additional gateway — manage everything directly from your phone.
