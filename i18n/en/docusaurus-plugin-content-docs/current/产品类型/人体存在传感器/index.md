# Occupancy/Presence Sensor

Occupancy/Presence sensors utilize **millimeter-wave radar technology** to detect stationary human presence, complementing traditional PIR infrared sensors.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Features |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| HS8OS | Zigbee | Millimeter-wave radar presence detection |

## Technical Principle

Unlike PIR sensors that can only detect movement, presence sensors transmit and receive millimeter-wave signals, leveraging the micro-Doppler effect to detect subtle body movements (such as chest movement from breathing), thereby determining if someone is present.

## Core Features

- **Stationary human detection**: detects lying, sitting still, and other subtle movements
- **Range detection**: covers a radius of 3-5 meters
- **Ceiling mount**: optimal detection angle
- **Complements PIR**: PIR handles wide-range motion detection, presence sensor handles fine-grained detection

## Scenario Examples

- **Smart lighting**: lights stay on while someone is present, auto-off when unoccupied (solves PIR lights-off-when-stationary problem)
- **AC control**: automatically turn on/off based on room occupancy
- **Elderly care**: alert system if elderly person remains stationary in bathroom/bedroom for too long
- **Office energy saving**: auto-adjust energy when meeting rooms/workstations are unoccupied
