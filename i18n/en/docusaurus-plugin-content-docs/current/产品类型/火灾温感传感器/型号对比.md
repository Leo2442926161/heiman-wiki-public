# Model Comparison

## Model Comparison

| Model | Protocol | Detection Principle | Power | Features | Recommended Use |
|-------|----------|-------------------|-------|----------|----------------|
| Heat Detector | Zigbee / Sub-1G | Thermistor (fixed temp + rate-of-rise) | Battery | Resists smoke interference | Kitchen/garage dedicated, alarm on abnormal temperature rise, auto-close gas solenoid valve |

## Usage Scenarios

| Scenario | Recommended Model | Automation Scheme |
|----------|------------------|-------------------|
| Kitchen safety | Heat Detector | Abnormal temperature rise → close gas valve → siren alarm → phone push, avoids false alarms from cooking smoke |
| Garage/boiler room | Heat Detector | Monitor equipment room temperature, prevent spontaneous combustion, activate exhaust fan for cooling |
| Complement smoke sensor | Heat Detector + Smoke | Smoke sensor covers whole house, heat detector covers kitchen/garage, complementary alarms for greater reliability |

## Selection Guide

- **Kitchen dedicated**: Heat Detector (avoids false alarms from cooking smoke)
- **Use with smoke sensor**: Smoke sensor covers bedrooms/living room, heat detector covers kitchen/garage
