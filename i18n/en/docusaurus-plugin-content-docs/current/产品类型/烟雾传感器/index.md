# Smoke Sensor

Heiman smoke sensors utilize **photoelectric smoke detection technology** to sensitively detect visible smoke particles produced in the early stages of a fire, enabling extremely early fire warning.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Certification | Target Market |
|--------|--------------|---------------|---------------|
| HS1SA Series | Zigbee / Z-Wave | EN 14604 | EU |
| HS3SA Series | Zigbee | EN 14604 | EU |
| HS8SA | Zigbee / Wi-Fi | UL 217 | US |
| S1 Series | Sub-1G Standalone | EN 14604 | General |
| HS1SA-M | Matter over Thread | EN 14604 | Global |

## Core Features

- Photoelectric smoke detection, capable of detecting smoke from smoldering and open flames
- 85 dB audible and visual alarm
- Low battery notification
- Self-test function (one-button test)
- Tamper alarm
- Remote silence support

## Technical Specifications (HS1SA-M as example)

- Sensor Type: Infrared Photoelectric
- Alarm Sound Pressure: ≥85 dB (at 3m)
- Power Supply: CR123A battery
- Battery Life: Approximately 3 years
- Operating Temperature: -10°C ~ +50°C
- Operating Humidity: ≤95% RH
