# Model Comparison

## Model Comparison

| Model | Protocol | Certification | Power | Market | Recommended Use |
|-------|----------|--------------|-------|--------|----------------|
| [HS1SA-E](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS1SA-E.md) | Zigbee | EN 14604 | CR123A | EU | Integrate with Zigbee2MQTT/ZHA, pair with Home Assistant. When smoke is detected, auto-turn on lights + push phone notification |
| [HS1SA-Z](../../无线协议/Zwave/HS1SA-Z.md) | Z-Wave | EN 14604 | CR123A | EU | Connect to SmartThings/Hubitat, pair with Z-Wave door sensor for away arm mode, smoke alarm auto-notification when away |
| [HS1SA-M](../../无线协议/Matter/HS1SA-M.md) | Matter over Thread | EN 14604 | CR123A | Global | Native integration with Apple Home/Google Home, no dedicated gateway needed, Thread low-latency local alarm automation |
| [HS3SA](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS3SA.md) | Zigbee | EN 14604 | CR123A | EU | Works with Heiman HS6GW gateway, multi-device scene automation, suitable for Heiman ecosystem users |
| [HS8SA](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS8SA.md) | Zigbee / Wi-Fi | UL 217 | Battery/Mains | US | US market dedicated, UL certified, meets local fire safety codes, supports WiFi direct or Zigbee gateway |
| [S1 Series](../../无线协议/Sub1G(long distance range)/S1系列.md) | Sub-1G | EN 14604 | Battery | General | Standalone interconnect, no gateway or internet required, multiple S1 units interconnect via Sub-1G, ideal for simple rental properties |

## Usage Scenarios

| Scenario | Recommended Model | Automation Scheme |
|----------|------------------|-------------------|
| Deep smart home integration | HS1SA-E / HS1SA-M | HA automation: smoke alarm → turn on all lights → unlock door → close gas valve → push phone notification |
| Apple HomeKit users | HS1SA-M | Native Matter integration, HomePod as Thread border router, automatic scene triggering |
| EU/US standard requirements | HS1SA-E (CE) / HS8SA (UL) | Meets local fire certification and insurance compliance requirements |
| No gateway / low cost | S1 Series | Ready to use out of the box, Sub-1G interconnect between multiple units, trigger each other on alarm |
| Existing Z-Wave system | HS1SA-Z | Compatible with existing Z-Wave gateways like Hubitat/SmartThings |

## Selection Guide

- **EU Market**: [HS1SA-E](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS1SA-E.md) (Zigbee) or [HS3SA](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS3SA.md) (Zigbee), choose [HS1SA-Z](../../无线协议/Zwave/HS1SA-Z.md) for Z-Wave
- **US Market**: [HS8SA](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS8SA.md) (UL 217 certified)
- **Cross-platform Matter**: [HS1SA-M](../../无线协议/Matter/HS1SA-M.md) (Matter over Thread)
- **Standalone scenario**: [S1 Series](../../无线协议/Sub1G(long distance range)/S1系列.md) (no gateway needed)
