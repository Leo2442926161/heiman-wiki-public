# Water Leakage Sensor

Water leakage sensors detect leaks or standing water, effectively preventing property damage from burst pipes and flooding.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Features |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| HS1WL | Zigbee | Basic model, probe detection |
| HS2WL Series | Zigbee | Probe + cable detection, dual channel |
| HS3WL Series | Zigbee | Enhanced model |
| L1 Series | Matter / Zigbee | Matter over Thread version, WWHA certified |

## Core Features

- Real-time water leak/standing water detection
- Buzzer alarm
- Low battery notification
- Anti-corrosion probe design

## Installation Locations

- Under bathroom vanities
- Under kitchen sinks
- Next to washing machines/dishwashers
- Below water heaters
- Basements/garages
- Near radiators

## Scene Automation

- **Leak detection + solenoid valve**: auto-close main water valve when leak is detected
- **Leak alarm + push notification**: notify property management or family via phone when away
- **Water leak + smart shutoff**: automate gas water heater or smart water valve
