# Temperature and Humidity Sensor

Temperature and humidity sensors provide real-time monitoring of ambient temperature and relative humidity, serving as essential devices for smart home environmental monitoring.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Features |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| H1 Series | Matter / Zigbee | Matter over Thread version WWHA certified |

## Core Features

- Real-time temperature and humidity monitoring
- Low battery notification
- Supports automation control (turn on AC when hot, turn on dehumidifier when humid, etc.)

## Technical Specifications

- Temperature Accuracy: ±0.3°C
- Humidity Accuracy: ±3% RH
- Power Supply: CR2 battery
- Battery Life: Approximately 2-3 years

## Scenario Examples

- Automate AC/heating to regulate room temperature
- Automate dehumidifier/humidifier to maintain comfortable humidity
- Monitor wine cellar/cigar cabinet environment
- Auto-control kitchen/bathroom exhaust fans
- Greenhouse environment monitoring
