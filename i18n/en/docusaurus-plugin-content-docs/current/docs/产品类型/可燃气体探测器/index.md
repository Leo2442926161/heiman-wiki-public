# Gas Detector

Gas detectors are used to detect leaks of combustible gases such as natural gas (methane) and liquefied petroleum gas (propane), making them essential devices for kitchen safety.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Detection Gas | Certifications |
|------|----------|----------|----------|

| JT-HS1CG Series | Zigbee | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 |
| JT-HS3CG | Zigbee | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 |
| JT-HS8CG | Zigbee / Wi-Fi | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 |

## Core Features

- High-stability semiconductor gas sensor
- Tiered concentration alarms
- Automatic shut-off联动 (with solenoid valve)
- Audible and visual alarm (≥85 dB)
- Self-test function
- Sensor end-of-life reminder

## Automation Scenarios

- **Leak detected → Close valve**: Automatically shut off the gas pipe valve
- **Alarm + Ventilation**: Automatically turn on exhaust fan / range hood
- **Push notification**: Receive real-time leak alerts on your phone

## Safety Tips

If you smell gas, do not operate electrical switches or use open flames. Immediately open windows for ventilation and evacuate to a safe outdoor area before calling for help.
