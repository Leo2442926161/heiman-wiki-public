# Model Comparison

## Model Comparison

| Model | Protocol | Detection Gas | Certification | Power | Features | Recommendation |
|------|------|----------|------|------|------|----------|
| JT-G1 | Standalone | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 | Mains | Standalone alarm | No gateway needed, built-in buzzer, basic gas leak alarm |
| [JT-HS1CG](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS1CG.md) | Zigbee | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 | Mains | Zigbee connected | Leak detected → Zigbee notification to phone → auto-close solenoid valve |
| [JT-HS3CG](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS3CG.md) | Zigbee | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 | Mains | Enhanced | Higher sensitivity, supports remote valve shut-off + alarm |
| JT-HS8CG | Zigbee / Wi-Fi | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 | Mains | Dual-mode | WiFi direct or Zigbee gateway, dual communication redundancy |
| [JT Series](../../无线协议/Zigbee/JT系列.md) | Zigbee | Natural gas/LPG | GB 15322 | Mains | Universal series | Multiple models available for different scenarios |

## Recommended Use Cases

| Scenario | Recommended Model | Automation Scheme |
|------|----------|----------|
| Kitchen safety automation | HS1CG / HS3CG | Leak detected → auto-close solenoid valve → turn on exhaust fan → push phone notification → trigger siren |
| Rental / Apartment | JT-G1 | Standalone alarm, no internet needed, low cost, meets fire inspection requirements |
| Remote monitoring | HS8CG | Dual communication + cloud platform remote status check |
| Commercial kitchen | HS3CG | High sensitivity + exhaust system联动 + property platform centralized management |

## Selection Guide

- **Standalone use**: JT-G1
- **Zigbee connected**: [HS1CG](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS1CG.md) or [HS3CG](../../无线协议/Zigbee/HS3CG.md)
- **Dual communication**: JT-HS8CG
