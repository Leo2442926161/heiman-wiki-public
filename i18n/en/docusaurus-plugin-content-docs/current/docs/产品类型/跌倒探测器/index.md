# Fall Detector

The Fall Down Detector is specially designed for the elderly. It automatically detects fall events and sends distress signals in time, making it a key device in smart elderly care solutions.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Features |
|------|----------|------|
| HS2FD | Zigbee | Smart fall detection, automatic alarm |

## Core Technology

It uses a **multi-sensor fusion algorithm**, combining accelerometer and gyroscope data with AI to determine fall posture, effectively distinguishing falls from normal activities (such as sitting down or bending over), reducing false alarm rates.

## Core Features

- Automatic fall detection
- One-touch manual求救
- Wearable design (belt clip or lanyard)
- Low battery reminder

## Applicable Scenarios

- Home care: 24/7 protection for elderly living alone
- Nursing homes: Centralized monitoring and management
- Rehabilitation centers: Fall risk monitoring for post-surgery patients
