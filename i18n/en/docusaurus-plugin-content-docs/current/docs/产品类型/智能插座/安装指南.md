# Installation Guide

## Installation Location Requirements

- Plug directly into a wall outlet, **do not daisy-chain** (do not plug into a power strip or extension cord)
- Ensure the outlet rating is ≥ 16A
- Do not install in damp or high-temperature environments

## Installation Steps

1. Plug the smart plug directly into a 220V (or 110V) wall outlet
2. The indicator light will turn on after power-up
3. Press and hold the button to enter pairing mode
4. Add the device in the Heiman app
5. Once paired successfully, remote on/off control is available

## Safety Precautions

- **Max load**: 16A / 3680W (EU standard), 15A / 1800W (US standard)
- **Do not overload**: Pay attention to power limits when using high-power appliances (AC, heaters)
- **Do not daisy-chain**: Do not plug the smart plug into a power strip
- Do not cover or obstruct the plug housing
- Periodically check the plug for overheating

## Typical Applications

- Timer control for low-power devices such as water heaters, humidifiers, aquarium lights
- Sensor联动: light on when someone arrives, off when they leave
- Energy monitoring: track appliance power consumption
