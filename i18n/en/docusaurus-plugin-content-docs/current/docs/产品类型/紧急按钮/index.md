# Emergency Button

The Emergency Button / Panic Button is used to quickly trigger a distress signal in emergency situations, making it an important component of elderly care and security systems.

## Product Series

| Series | Communication | Features |
|------|----------|------|
| Emergency Button | Zigbee | Portable, one-touch求救 |

## Core Features

- One-touch emergency alarm
- Portable design (carry-on or wall-mounted)
- Low battery reminder
- Anti-accidental activation (long press to trigger)

## Applicable Scenarios

- **Elderly care**: One-touch求救 when elderly person suddenly falls ill or falls
- **Children's room**: Child home alone can use in emergencies
- **Medical care**: Bedside call button in hospital rooms / nursing homes
- **Retail/front desk**: Covert alarm in case of robbery or emergency

## Trigger 联动

- Push notification to family members / caregivers
- 联动 siren
- Auto-call property management or emergency services
