# About Heiman

## About Heiman

Shenzhen Heiman Technology Co., Ltd. (Heiman) was founded in 2005 and is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the field of smart home security. Headquartered in Shenzhen, China, the company has over 20 years of industry experience, with products exported to Europe, the Americas, Asia, and many other countries and regions worldwide.

## Core Business

Heiman's mission is "Safeguarding with craftsmanship, warming life with technology." Its main business areas include:

- **Smart Fire Safety**: Smoke alarms, carbon monoxide alarms, gas detectors, heat detectors, etc.
- **Smart Gas Safety**: Gas leak detection and remote shut-off solutions
- **Smart Elderly Care**: Fall detection, emergency call, and other elderly safety monitoring solutions
- **Smart Sensing**: PIR motion sensors, presence sensors, door/window sensors, temperature/humidity sensors, water leak sensors, air quality sensors, and more

## Technical Strength

Heiman is an early adopter and promoter of the **Matter protocol**, having launched one of the world's first Matter-certified carbon monoxide alarms. Products support multiple wireless protocols:

- **Matter over Thread** — The latest smart home interoperability standard
- **Zigbee 3.0** — Mature low-power mesh network
- **Z-Wave** — Mainstream smart home protocol in Europe and America
- **Wi-Fi** — Direct connection, no gateway needed
- **Sub-1G** — Ultra-long range low-power communication
- **wM-Bus** — Wireless metering bus

## Ecosystem Partnerships

Heiman products are fully integrated with major smart home platforms, including Home Assistant, Apple HomeKit, Google Home, Amazon Alexa, SmartThings, Homey, OpenHAB, Zigbee2MQTT, ZHA, Tuya, and more, and have received official **Works with Home Assistant** certification.

## Certifications & Qualifications

- Works with Home Assistant Official Certified Partner (2026)
- Matter certified devices
- EN 14604 (European standard for smoke alarms)
- EN 50291 (European standard for CO alarms)
- UL 217 / UL 2034 (US standards)
