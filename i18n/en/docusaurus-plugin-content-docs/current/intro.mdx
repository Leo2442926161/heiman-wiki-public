# About Heiman Technology

**Shenzhen Heiman Technology Co., Ltd.** was founded in 2005. It is a **National High-Tech Enterprise** and a **National Specialized and New "Little Giant" Enterprise** integrating independent R&D, production, sales, and service.

## About Heiman

Heiman Technology focuses on the R&D and innovation of smart sensor products, covering:

- **Smart Fire Safety** — Smoke alarms, CO alarms, and fire safety sensors
- **Smart Gas Safety** — Gas leak alarms, emergency gas shutoff valves
- **Smart Elderly Care** — Emergency call buttons, fall detectors, water leak detectors
- **Smart Sensors** — Infrared detectors, door/window sensors, temperature/humidity sensors
