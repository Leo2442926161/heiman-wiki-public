# Creating Blog Posts

The Heiman Technology Blog publishes company news, industry trends, product reviews, and technical sharing.

## How to Contribute

Heiman team members and partners are welcome to contribute articles in the following areas:

- Smart fire/gas/elderly care industry trend analysis
- Heiman product use cases and reviews
- In-depth technical whitepapers and solution analysis
- Company news and event coverage

Please contact the marketing department or email us for submissions. Articles will be published on the blog after review.
