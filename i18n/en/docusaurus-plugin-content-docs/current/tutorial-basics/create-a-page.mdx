# Products

Heiman Technology offers four core product lines covering Smart Fire Safety, Smart Gas Safety, Smart Elderly Care, and Smart Sensors.

## Smart Fire Safety

Includes smoke alarms, carbon monoxide alarms, heat detectors, etc., supporting cellular network (4G/NB-IoT) connectivity for easy integration with third-party IoT platforms.

Key Features:

- Full network compatibility, multi-platform integration
- Compliant with EN54-7, UL 217 and other international standards
- Supports Matter protocol, compatible with Alexa, Google Home, Apple HomeKit

## Smart Gas Safety

Industrial and commercial smart gas alarm systems, providing comprehensive intelligent gas safety solutions for various environments.

Key Features:

- High-precision gas detection sensors
- Real-time remote monitoring and alarm
- Automatic联动 with emergency gas shutoff valves

## Smart Elderly Care

Includes smoke alarms, gas alarms, water leak detectors, fall sensors, door security devices, and emergency alarms.

Key Features:

- AI-based fall detection algorithm
- One-key emergency call
- Multi-device notification to family and service agencies

## Smart Sensors

Covering full-scenario sensors including infrared detection, door/window magnetic sensing, temperature/humidity monitoring, and air quality detection.

Key Features:

- Ultra-low power design
- Supports Zigbee / Matter / WiFi multi-protocol
- Compatible with major smart home platforms
