# Certifications

## Applicable Standards

- [CE EU Conformity](../../../../../认证标准/CE%20欧盟符合性.md)
- [Other Certifications](../../../../认证标准/其他认证.md) — RoHS, Z-Wave Plus Certified, S2 Security

## Standard Descriptions

- **CE**: Mandatory conformity marking for products sold in the European Economic Area, covering RED and EMC directives.
- **FCC**: US regulatory requirement for electromagnetic emissions.
- **RoHS**: Restriction of Hazardous Substances directive for electronic products.
- **Z-Wave Plus**: Certified Z-Wave Plus device with full backward compatibility.
- **S2 Security**: Z-Wave S2 authenticated encryption for secure communications.

For more details, please refer to the respective certification standard pages.
