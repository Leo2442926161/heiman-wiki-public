# S1 Series - Standalone Smoke Alarm (Sub-1G)

## Overview

The S1 series is a standalone Sub-1G smoke alarm from Heiman, operating in the 433 MHz / 868 MHz frequency band, using photoelectric smoke detection technology, and certified to EN 14604. No gateway or internet is required — devices interlink directly via Sub-1G RF. When any device triggers an alarm, all devices in the same group alarm simultaneously, suitable for large-area fire safety scenarios such as buildings, shopping malls, and hotels.

## Features

- Photoelectric smoke detection, detects smoldering and open flame smoke
- Wireless interlinked alarm: when any device in the network triggers, all devices sound alarm simultaneously
- Standalone operation: no gateway, no internet required, direct device-to-device communication
- 85 dB loud sound and light alarm
- Low battery notification
- Self-test function (one-button test)
- Tamper alarm
- Supports remote silence
- Ultra-low power design, battery life over 5 years

## Certification Standards
