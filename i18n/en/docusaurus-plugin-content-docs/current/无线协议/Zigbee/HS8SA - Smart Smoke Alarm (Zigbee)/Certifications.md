# Certifications

## Applicable Standards

- [UL US Standards](../../../../认证标准/UL%20美国保险商实验室.md) — UL 217

## Standard Descriptions

- **UL 217**: US standard for residential smoke alarms. Covers photoelectric smoke detection, specific fire curve tests (smoldering and flaming), and alarm sound level requirements. This product is designed for the US market.

For more details, please refer to the respective certification standard pages.
