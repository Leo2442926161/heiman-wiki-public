# Certifications

## Applicable Standards

- [GB Chinese National Standards](../../../../认证标准/GB%20中国国家标准.md) — GB 15322
- [CCC China Compulsory Certification](../../../../认证标准/CCC%20中国强制认证.md)

## Standard Descriptions

- **GB 15322.2**: Chinese national standard for household combustible gas detectors — the JT series shut-off valve is certified as part of the gas detection and shut-off system.
- **CCC**: China Compulsory Certification for gas safety products sold in the Chinese market.

For more details, please refer to the respective certification standard pages.
