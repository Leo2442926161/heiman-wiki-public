# Certifications

## Applicable Standards

- [EN European Standards](../../../../认证标准/EN%20欧洲标准.md) — EN 14604
- [CE EU Conformity](../../../../认证标准/CE%20欧盟符合性.md)
- [Other Certifications](../../../认证标准/其他认证.md) — Q-Label, Red Dot Design Award

## Standard Descriptions

- **EN 14604**: European standard for standalone smoke alarms. Covers photoelectric smoke detection, alarm sound level (≥85 dB at 3 m), battery low indication, temperature cycling, humidity endurance, and RF interference immunity.
- **CE / RED**: Compliance with Radio Equipment Directive (2014/53/EU) for wireless devices.
- **Q-Label**: Zigbee interoperability quality certification.
- **Red Dot Design Award**: International product design award.

For more details, please refer to the respective certification standard pages.
