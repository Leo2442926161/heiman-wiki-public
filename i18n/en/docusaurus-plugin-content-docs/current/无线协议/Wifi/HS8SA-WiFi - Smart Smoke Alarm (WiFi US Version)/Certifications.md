# Certifications

## Applicable Standards

- [UL US Standards](../../../../认证标准/UL%20美国保险商实验室.md) — UL 217
- [Other Certifications](../../../认证标准/其他认证.md) — FCC, NFPA 72

## Standard Descriptions

- **UL 217**: US standard for residential smoke alarms. Covers photoelectric smoke detection, specific fire curve tests (smoldering and flaming), and alarm sound level requirements.
- **FCC**: US regulatory requirement for electromagnetic emissions and wireless devices.
- **NFPA 72**: National Fire Alarm and Signaling Code compliance for interconnected fire alarm systems.

For more details, please refer to the respective certification standard pages.
