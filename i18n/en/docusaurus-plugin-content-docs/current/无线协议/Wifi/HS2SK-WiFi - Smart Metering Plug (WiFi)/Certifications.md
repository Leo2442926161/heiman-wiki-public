# Certifications

## Applicable Standards

- [CE EU Conformity](../../../../认证标准/CE%20欧盟符合性.md)
- [UL US Standards](../../../../认证标准/UL%20美国保险商实验室.md) — UL / ETL

## Standard Descriptions

- **CE**: Mandatory conformity marking for products sold in the European Economic Area.
- **FCC**: US regulatory requirement for electromagnetic emissions.
- **ETL / UL**: US safety certification for electrical products (depending on market version).
- **RoHS**: Restriction of Hazardous Substances directive for electronic products.

For more details, please refer to the respective certification standard pages.
