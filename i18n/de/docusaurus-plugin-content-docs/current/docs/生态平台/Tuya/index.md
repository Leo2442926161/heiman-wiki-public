#Tuya (Tuya)

Tuya (Tuya Smart) is the world's leading IoT cloud platform, and Heiman is one of its important ecological partners. Through the Tuya ecosystem, Heiman devices can quickly connect to 1,000+ brands of smart home systems.

## Integrationsmethode

### Smart Life App

Hyman Zigbee devices can be configured and controlled via Tuya’s Smart Life App:

1. Prepare Tuya-compatible Zigbee gateway (such as Heiman HS6GW series)
2. Öffnen Sie die Smart Life App → Gerät hinzufügen
3. Wählen Sie Zigbee-Gateway → Untergerät suchen
4. Put the sensor into pairing mode to automatically discover

### Plattformübergreifende Unterstützung

Through the Tuya ecosystem, Heiman equipment can be linked with the following platforms:

-Amazon Alexa
- Google Assistant
- Apple Siri-Verknüpfungen
- IFTTT

### Lokalisiertes Tuya-Gateway

With Tuya Local, like Home Assistant’s Tuya Local integration, Heiman devices can be controlled directly locally, reducing latency in the cloud.

## Anwendungsbereich

Tuya's compatible Heiman product line includes: smoke sensors, carbon monoxide sensors, door sensors, human body infrared, temperature and humidity, water immersion, sockets and other Zigbee devices.
